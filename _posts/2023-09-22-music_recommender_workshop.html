Music Recommender Systems workshop at NUS

 

The Music Recommender Systems Workshop was a seminar event on 22 September 2023 at NUS School of Computing. We are honor to have Prof. Peter Knees from Vienna University of Technology, Karlijn Dinnissen from Utrecht University, and Prof. Doug Turnbull from Ithaca College to share their research. This seminar was chaired Prof Ye Wang and was open to faculties and students of School of Computing.

[01] A mini-tutorial on music recommender systems

Presentation by Peter Knees, Vienna University of Technology.

[02] Item Provider Perspectives on Influence and Fairness of Music Streaming Platforms

Presentation by Karlijn Dinnissen, Utrecht University.

[03] Popularity Degradation Bias in Local Music Recommendation

Presentation by Doug Turnbull, Ithaca College.
